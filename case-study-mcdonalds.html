McDonald's

Designing a playbook that turns road loops into campaign-ready assets, exported in all ratios, tagged by route and sign type, and easy to find.

Our Process

From Planning to Capture
A systematic approach to visual documentation

01

Identify Problems

Fragmented coverage and inconsistent standards limited reuse and credibility.

Existing McDonald's assets were scattered, outdated, and concentrated in a few markets, leaving gaps along key interstates and in the Midwest. Angles, lighting, and resolution varied by shooter, undermining both efficiency and brand consistency.

02

Discover Opportunities

Data-led routing revealed high-yield loops and a repeatable capture playbook.

A Geo analysis of location density and drive-time surfaced a 3-day "Midwest Loop", that maximized architectural and regional variety per mile. We defined a standard approach: ground + aerial capture, golden/blue-hour windows, a fixed shot ladder, and a metadata schema (route, state, city, roadway, sign type, conditions) to make every file instantly discoverable.

03

Refine the Creative Vision

A searchable, on-brand visual system that scales with every route.

We delivered a library designed for real use: 78 high-resolution photographs spanning multiple states and architectural styles. The result is a consistent, campaign-ready resource for decks, internal documentation, and marketing, built to grow as new routes are added.
