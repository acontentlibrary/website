SCA Health

Creating a content ecosystem that spotlights employee stories and unifies company values into compelling visual narratives.

Our Process

From Challenge to Content
A systematic approach to storytelling

01

Identify Problems

We analyzed SCA Health's content challenges, from employee engagement gaps to inconsistent brand messaging across departments.

Through stakeholder interviews and content audits, we identified key pain points: lack of authentic employee voices, disconnected values messaging, and limited recruitment content.

02

Discover Opportunities

We uncovered powerful stories within SCA Health's workforce and identified key moments to amplify their company values.

Career-journey narratives emerged as the backbone of the concept. We surfaced day-in-the-life arcs across clinical, center, and corporate teams that show how mentorship, training, and cross-functional collaboration open real growth paths.

Each story ties a tangible milestone; first certification, leadership step, patient win; back to SCA Health's values (Clinical Quality, Service Excellence, Teamwork, Inclusion), so development and culture sit in the same frame.

The result: recruiting content that feels authentic to employees while clearly signaling advancement, belonging, and purpose to candidates.

03

Refine Creative Vision

We crafted a unified content ecosystem that transforms individual stories into a cohesive brand narrative.

We built a modular content catalog that scales: a flagship values film to introduce the culture; a series of career-journey spotlights; short social cut-downs; a stills library; and branded motion/graphic templates. The system plugs into recruitment campaigns, onboarding, town halls, and brand channels one capture feeding many placements so teams can deploy consistent, on-message content across internal communications and external marketing without reshoots.
